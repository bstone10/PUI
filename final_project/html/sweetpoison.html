Sweet Poison

Ingredients:

- 1 oz light rum
- 2 oz coconut rum
- 1 oz blue curaçao
- Pineapple juice
- Pineapple wedge

Time: 6 minutes
Difficulty: Difficult

Directions:

1. Combine both rums and curaçao in cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake until cold and then pour into cocktail glass.

2. Fill rest of glass with chilled pineapple juice. Garnish with pineapple wedge.
