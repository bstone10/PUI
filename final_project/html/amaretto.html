Amaretto Sidecar

Ingredients:

- 1 oz amaretto liquor
- 1 oz brandy
- 1 oz lemon juice
- 2.5 oz pitted cherries

Time: 5 minutes
Difficulty: Medium

Directions:

1. Combine amaretto, brandy, lemon juice, and cherries is a blender and purée until mostly smooth, about 1-2 minutes.

2. Fill a 14 ounce glass with the finely crushed or shaved ice and top with the puréed cherry mixture.
