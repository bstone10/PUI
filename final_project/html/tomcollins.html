Tom Collins

Ingredients:

- 2 oz gin
- 0.75 oz lemon juice
- 0.5 oz simple syrup
- 2 oz club soda
- 1 lemon wedge

Time: 5 minutes
Difficulty: Medium

Directions:

1. Combine gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker.

2. Strain into your glass, top with club soda, and garnish with a lemon wedge.
