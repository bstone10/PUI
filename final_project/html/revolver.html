Revolver

Ingredients:

- 2 oz bourbon
- 0.5 oz coffee liqueur
- 2 dashes of orange bitters
- Orange peel

Time: 3 minutes
Difficulty: Beginner

Directions:

1. Add all the ingredients to a mixing glass filled with ice, and stir until chilled.

2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass, and garnish with a wide strip of orange peel.
